# What is the Science Generalization Hypothesis (SGH)?

*Published on March 1, 2025*

The **Science Generalization Hypothesis (SGH)** is a foundational ontological framework that explains the origin and structure of all existence without relying on any physical cosmological model.

At its core, SGH proposes that our physical reality emerges from a non-spatiotemporal **Metaphysical World (Ψ)** through a lawful extraction process. This process generates dimensional matrices, which are then segmented and instantiated as physical entities, each manifesting two irreducible aspects: **quantity** (object in space) and **quality** (motion in time).

SGH resolves long-standing paradoxes in physics and mathematics, unifies abstract and concrete existence, and provides a coherent bridge between metaphysics and modern science. It is not a physical theory but the ontological framework that makes any physical theory possible.
